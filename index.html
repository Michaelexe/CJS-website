WHY COACH JULES

JJulien Brown-Latham, a strength and conditioning specialist with over 12 years of experience, has worked with athletes from youth to professional levels.

He played Division I and II soccer while earning degrees in Rehabilitation Science and Kinesiology.

Julien is now the Director of Wellness and Sports Performance at Kings Cross Physiotherapy & Sports Injury Clinic and Head of Strength and Conditioning for Athlete's Institute Football Club in Mono, Ontario.

Enhanced Performance:

Strength and conditioning programs designed to improve athletic performance by increasing strength, speed, agility, and endurance, helping you perform at your best when called upon.

Injury Prevention:

By building a strong foundation, strength and conditioning can help reduce your risk of injuries, which allows you to train and compete more safely and consistently.

Confidence and Resilience:

Through customized programming, you will develop not only strength but also mental toughness, confidence and resilience which will benefit you both on and off the field.
