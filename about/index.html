Get to know Coach Jules

Julien Brown-Latham

From the moment I kicked a soccer ball at age 4, I was hooked. My dream was to become a professional player, and everything I did was aimed at that goal. But life threw challenges my way, including tearing my ACL twice, with the second injury happening just before I started university in the USA. It was a tough setback, but it taught me resilience, patience, and the value of perseverance.

As a former D1 and D2 player, I learned what it takes to compete at a high level and was fortunate to gain insights from top coaches, even during times when I was sidelined. That experience deepened my love for the game and taught me to never take my time on the field for granted.

Transitioning to coaching felt like a natural next step. I wanted to pass on my passion for soccer and help the next generation of athletes succeed. My focus on strength and conditioning grew from a desire to keep athletes healthy, so they can seize their moment when it counts.
